Do I need a referral from my primary care physician to visit your clinic?
No, New York State law allows for direct access to chiropractic care without a physician referral. However, if you plan to use insurance, some plans may require a referral for coverage. We recommend calling our office at the time of booking, and our staff can help verify your specific insurance requirements.
How long does a typical chiropractic session take at your Valley Stream location?
Your first visit typically takes 45-60 minutes, including paperwork, consultation, examination, and initial treatment. Follow-up visits usually last 20-30 minutes, depending on your treatment plan. We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early for your first appointment to complete necessary paperwork, though you can also find these forms on our website to fill out in advance.
What types of conditions do you treat at Well Being Chiropractic PC?
We treat a wide range of conditions including lower back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, sports injuries, and work-related injuries. Our approach combines traditional chiropractic adjustments with massage therapy and therapeutic exercises. We commonly help local Valley Stream residents with commuter-related issues from long train rides to Manhattan and desk-work related problems.
Is there anything I should or shouldn't do before my first chiropractic adjustment?
For the best experience, wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement. Avoid heavy meals 2-3 hours before your appointment. Bring any recent X-rays or MRI reports if you have them, along with a list of current medications. If you're coming straight from work in the city, we recommend bringing a change of clothes. Also, please arrive hydrated, as this helps with both the adjustment and any massage therapy you might receive.
