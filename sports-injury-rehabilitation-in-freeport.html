How long will my sports injury rehabilitation treatment take?
Treatment duration varies depending on your specific injury and its severity. For most sports-related injuries, patients typically see significant improvement within 6-8 weeks of consistent treatment. We create customized treatment plans that usually involve 2-3 visits per week initially, decreasing as you improve. For example, a typical sprained ankle rehabilitation program at our Freeport clinic includes 4-6 weeks of treatment, while more complex injuries like rotator cuff issues may require 8-12 weeks. We regularly assess your progress and adjust the treatment timeline accordingly.
