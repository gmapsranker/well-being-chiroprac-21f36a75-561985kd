Frequently Asked Questions About Spinal Decompression Therapy

How much does spinal decompression therapy cost at Well Being Chiropractic PC?
The cost of spinal decompression therapy at our Lynbrook office typically ranges from $75-125 per session, with most treatment plans requiring 12-20 sessions for optimal results. We accept most major insurance plans, which may cover a portion of the treatment. We also offer package pricing and payment plans to help make treatment more affordable. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed treatment plan with exact costs based on your specific condition and insurance coverage.
How long does each spinal decompression session take, and how often will I need treatment?
Each spinal decompression session at our Lynbrook clinic lasts approximately 30-45 minutes. A typical treatment protocol involves 2-3 sessions per week for 4-6 weeks, though this can vary based on your specific condition and response to treatment. The sessions themselves are non-invasive and painless, with most patients reporting they're quite relaxing. We recommend arriving 5-10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time, and you can usually return to normal activities immediately after treatment.
What conditions can be treated with spinal decompression at your clinic?
At Well Being Chiropractic PC, we successfully treat various conditions with spinal decompression, including herniated or bulging discs, sciatica, degenerative disc disease, and chronic lower back pain. However, not everyone is a candidate for this treatment. Patients with severe osteoporosis, spinal fractures, surgical fusion hardware, or advanced degenerative joint disease may need alternative treatments. During your initial consultation, we'll perform a thorough evaluation to determine if spinal decompression is the right option for your specific condition.
Do I need a referral from my primary care physician to receive treatment at Well Being Chiropractic PC?
No, you don't need a referral to receive treatment at our Lynbrook office. New York State allows direct access to chiropractic care. However, if you're planning to use insurance, some plans may require a referral for coverage. We recommend checking with your insurance provider regarding their specific requirements. We're happy to work with your primary care physician and can provide them with treatment updates if desired.
What should I expect during my first visit to Well Being Chiropractic PC?
Your first visit to our Lynbrook office will take about 60-90 minutes.
